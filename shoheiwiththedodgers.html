Shohei's First Spring Training Game with the Dodgers

Shohei Ohtani, The Los Angeles Dodgers 2-way player, made his debut with the team on February 27, 2024. In it's entirety, Shohei went 2-3 with his at bats, both hits being an RBI, one being a homerun. Although his first at bat was a strike out, he made his comeback to show everyone what he's capable of. Sadly, Shohei was unable to demonstrate his 2-way abilities due to an elbow injury he adapted during his last season with the Los Angeles Angels. Shohei will be away from pitching until 2025.

Through it all, Shohei is able to work on what he can improve on to help benefit the team. Most major league players are perfect examples of team players; Shohei being one of them.
